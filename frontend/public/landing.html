Common questions

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?
Not at all. If you can use email and a smartphone, you can use LitterDesk. Most breeders are fully set up within 10 minutes.
Are the AI contracts legally binding?
The contracts are informed by real legal templates and are digitally signed by both parties. We always recommend a local attorney review for peace of mind, but they're substantially more thorough than most breeder contracts in use today.
How does deposit collection work?
Through Stripe Connect. You onboard your bank account once, then send buyers a secure payment link. Funds transfer to your bank in 2 business days. LitterDesk takes a 1.5% platform fee only on deposits collected (Stripe's standard fee applies separately).
Can I import my existing buyer list?
Yes — we support CSV import for buyer contacts. Most breeders migrate their entire spreadsheet in under 5 minutes using our import wizard.
What breeds does this work for?
All breeds. The platform is breed-agnostic. The AI contract generator knows breed-specific health testing requirements for the 50 most popular AKC breeds.
What happens when my trial ends?
You'll be prompted to choose a plan. All your data stays intact. If you don't upgrade, you move to a read-only free tier — nothing gets deleted.
