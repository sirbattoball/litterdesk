# LitterDesk — 30-Day Launch Plan

## Brand
- **Name:** LitterDesk
- **Domain:** litterdesk.com (~$12/year on Namecheap)
- **Tagline:** "Run your kennel like a real business"
- **Colors:** Green (#15803d) + Warm white — approachable, professional
- **Tone:** Knowledgeable breeder-friend, not corporate software

## Week 2: Soft Launch (Days 8–14)

### Distribution (Organic — FREE)
These are the highest-converting channels for breeders. Work them in order:

**Facebook Groups (highest ROI)**
Target groups with 10k+ members. Join 10–15:
- "Dog Breeders of America" (285k members)
- "[Breed] Breeders" groups for top 20 AKC breeds
- "AKC Breeders" community

Post angle: "I built a free tool to replace the spreadsheets. 14-day trial. No card required."
Do NOT post spam. Provide value first: answer questions, then mention LitterDesk naturally.

**Reddit**
- r/dogs, r/dogbreeding, r/AKC
- Answer questions genuinely. Link in profile bio.

**Direct Outreach**
- Find 50 breeders on AKC marketplace whose listings mention "text for availability"
- These are your highest-intent leads (overwhelmed by demand)
- Personal DM with genuine offer

---

## Week 3: Convert & Iterate (Days 15–21)

### Goal: 10 paying customers

**Onboarding White-Glove**
- For first 10 customers: personally onboard each one via Zoom/phone
- This reveals friction, builds testimonials, and locks in retention
- Take notes on every complaint/confusion → fix immediately

**Referral Kickstart**
- Offer: "Refer a breeder friend, get one month free"
- Breeders know other breeders. Word of mouth is king in this niche.

**First Testimonials**
- After week 2, ask your 5 happiest users for a 1-sentence quote
- Add to landing page and social

---

## Week 4: Scale (Days 22–30)

### Goal: 25 paying customers → ~$1,975/mo

**Paid Ads (small budget)**
- Facebook/Instagram: $10/day targeting:
  - Interest: "dog breeding", "AKC", specific breed clubs
  - Behavior: small business owners
  - Age: 30–65 (core breeder demographic)
- Ad creative: Before/after — "From spreadsheets to this" screenshots
- Landing page: dedicated /litters or /contracts landing pages, not homepage

**Content SEO**
- Target keywords: "dog breeder software", "puppy sale contract template", "litter tracking app"
- Volume is low (500–2k/mo) but conversion intent is HIGH
- One 1,500-word post per week

**YouTube / TikTok**
- "Day in the life of a dog breeder" collaboration with micro-influencers (5k–50k followers)
- Sponsor with LitterDesk for $200–500 per video
- "I tried this dog breeder software for 30 days" format

## 6-Month Growth Levers

1. **Public litter listings** — Give each breeder a `/kennelname` public page showing available litters. This drives SEO AND gives breeders a reason to share LitterDesk with buyers.

2. **Buyer-facing portal** — Let buyers log in, see their waitlist position, upload documents, and sign contracts. Reduces back-and-forth significantly.

3. **Breed club partnerships** — Reach out to AKC parent clubs (Golden Retriever Club of America, etc.) for endorsed tool status. One email to a club newsletter = 500 targeted breeders.

4. **API for high-volume kennels** — Kennels managing 20+ litters/year will pay $300+/mo for API access.

5. **Marketplace expansion** — Once you have 500 breeders, launch buyer-facing search: "Find a reputable breeder." Charge breeders $10–30/mo to be listed. New revenue stream.
