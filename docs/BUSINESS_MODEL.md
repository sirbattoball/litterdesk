# LitterDesk — Business Model

## SaaS Pricing Tiers

| Plan        | Price/mo | Target                        | Key Limits              |
|-------------|----------|-------------------------------|-------------------------|
| Starter     | $29/mo   | Hobby breeders, 1-2 litters   | 2 active litters        |
| Pro         | $79/mo   | Semi-pro, 3-8 litters         | Unlimited litters, AI   |
| Kennel      | $149/mo  | Multi-breed, show kennels     | Multi-user, white-label |

## Path to $5,000/Month

| Milestone    | Users Needed                       | MRR      |
|--------------|------------------------------------|----------|
| Month 1-2    | 20 Pro ($79)                       | $1,580   |
| Month 3      | 40 Pro + 10 Starter                | $3,450   |
| Month 4-5    | 50 Pro + 15 Starter + 3 Kennel     | $5,082   |
| Month 6+     | Scale with content + referrals     | $8,000+  |

## Revenue Streams
1. Subscription (primary) — 80% of revenue
2. Transaction fee on deposits collected (1.5% via Stripe Connect) — 15%
3. Partner referrals (genetic testing labs, insurance) — 5%

## Key Differentiators
- AI that writes breed-specific sale contracts
- Buyer pipeline CRM with automated follow-up emails
- Mobile-first design (breeders work from phones)
- Stripe-native deposit collection built in
